Learn About Me

I am an MBBS graduate from B. P. Koirala Institute of Health Sciences (Class of 2025) with a passion for evidence-based primary care and equitable access to medicine. My clinical training across Dharan, Kathmandu, and rural outreach clinics strengthened my commitment to delivering empathetic patient journeys rooted in cultural humility and scientific rigor.

Beyond the wards, I invest in research on non-communicable diseases, lead community health education drives, and mentor future medical aspirants. I enjoy bridging compassionate bedside care with data-driven insights to build healthier communities in Nepal and beyond.
