Integrated Primary Care

Comprehensive services for every family member

Shanti Care Clinic in Kathmandu, Nepal, is designed as a calming, patient-first environment that blends evidence-based medicine with attentive listening. We support families through preventive screening, acute care, and chronic disease management delivered by Dr. Karan Kumar Yadav and a multidisciplinary team.

Modern diagnostics, digital follow-up tools, and community outreach programmes ensure that every visit remains personalised and culturally attuned.

What We Offer

Clinic services at a glance

Family Consultations

Comprehensive evaluations for all age groups with coordinated care plans and clear follow-up notes.

Chronic Disease Clinics

Dedicated slots for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and cardiac risk optimisation with lifestyle coaching.

Maternal & Child Health

Antenatal check-ups, vaccination counselling, and developmental milestone assessments for growing families.

Point-of-care Diagnostics

On-site vitals monitoring, ECG, glucometry, and rapid tests with referral pathways for advanced imaging.

Telehealth Reviews

Secure digital consultations for national and overseas patients with encrypted documentation.

Community Outreach

School health screenings, corporate wellness drives, and public health education tailored to Kathmandu wards.
